Our Interactive lectures keep students engaged in classes

Tutorials

Our specially designed tutorials helps to groom critical thinking and comprehension skills of the students. This is where the concepts learned in lectures are applied. We provide specialized tutorial sheets for students to practice based on the concepts learned from the interactive lectures. It is a crucial aspect of the learning experience which helps to hone the problem-solving skills of the students. We take the time to understand and solve different problems using a variety of approaches. Tutorials are intended to help students gain a deeper understanding of the subject matter which enables their understanding to see the implications and significance of their knowledge and apply it the right contexts. It fosters a healthy relationship between the teachers and students, thereby personalizing the entire experience of the students and supporting their personal career development in general.

Tutorials ensure that students are well versed with the course content

Regular Assessments

Assessments are conducted at regular intervals which help students to achieve consistency, create a better understanding, test their grasp of concepts, and develop the mindset required to face exams. It goes a long way to fulfill our Vision of increasing the competition level among students. Besides helping the students, the assessments helps us to gauge the student's learning curve so that we can adjust our pace to their needs. It helps us to identify their strengths and weaknesses. Assessments involve an ongoing process of analysis and reflection on evidence, and to make consistent and informed judgments with an aim of improving the studentâ€™s future learning experiences.

Feedback

A comprehensive analysis of assessments is provided to parents and students. This feedback clealy details the learning progress of the students. When imparting feedback to students and parents, we engage in dialogues which helps the students in the process of self assessment. This approach encourages learners to take responsibility for their learning. We are articulate and precise when providing feedback to ensure that parents and students are well informed and thus, enabling them to take the best corrective action as suited to their requirements.
With these coaching strategies, we ensure that quality learning practices are implemented accurately and taking our students to the limits of achievement.
